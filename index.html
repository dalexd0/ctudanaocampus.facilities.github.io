Hometel

Hometel, Shake the hustle and bustle from the city and come to this 16-room unassuming oasis within the University where you can relax and enjoy a 360-degree view of the Northeastern Cebu landscape or take a dip in the newly installed swimming pool and gaze over the eastern coastline by sunrise.

CTU Fitness Gym

CTU Fitness Gym, "Sweating is a good thing." This tagline creates the need to have a fitness center. Wellness and fitness are a recognized need in CTU Danao. Exercising regularly does not just make one physically fit but mentally sound well.

book now

Student Activity Center

Student Activity Center, The student activity center features a cafe-style foyer for collaboration and socialization, connecting to a newly-established outdoor open space as dance and theater rehearsals and flexible meeting bays. Purposely built to host multiple campus programs, this 400-sqm facility also provides a new venue for university-wide gatherings.
